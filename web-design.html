Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I start when building a website?

The first step when looking to build a website is asking "why". Having a clear reason such as "I'd like to increase sales", or "My business needs an online presence to produce more awareness" is a great starting point to base all design and functionality aspects of building a site.

Do you provide hosting services?

Absolutely. We offer online hosting services that include basic site hosting through the platform of your choice, with monthly maintenance on your site and regular security upkeep by our developers.

How much does a website cost?

Websites can cost anywhere from $1,500 to $10,000 depending on the way it's developed, functionality needed, design complexity, type of site, and more. A basic one page static site would be the lowest, while an online shopping platform with 1,000 different products would be on the high-end.

What are wireframes?

Wireframes are the building blocks used when creating a site, or application. A wireframe details how an online user will go from point A to point B, with simplified screens in-between.

How do I make my website responsive to different screen widths?

Making a website responsive online involves either creating media queries that connect a specific design to a maximum screen width, or going into your builder platform like Wordpress and adjusting options for tablet and mobile resolutions. Either way, making sure your website is responsive in 2023 is a must. If you need help with making your site work responsively, contact us below.

Do you offer SEO services?

We offer SEO audits and solutions to insure your website has the highest quality ranking factors in Google. These include Schema implementations, keyword specific content headings, managing html to text ratios, writing content, resolving security issues, making sure your site is mobile friendly, adding analytics and tracking, clean linking structure, backlinks and more.

How long does it take for SEO to make a difference?

Seeing results from SEO changes on your website will take time. It usually takes 4-12 months for Google and other search engines to verify your website's credibility, and push those SEO changes onto the front page. So don't expect your sparkly new website to rank on the first page right off the bat. Factors like keywords, competition, inbound links, and domain age also have an affect on how long it takes to see results.
